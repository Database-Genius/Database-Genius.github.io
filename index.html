If you are trying to contact Preston Hunter because you need a world-class database designer and programmer... Then you have come to the right place.

The data is the thing.

I have over 20 years of experience as an expert relational database designer. I currently favor MySQL and PHP for most new projects, but I have extensive experience with a wide variety of RDBMS’s and programming languages.

You can contact me at: preston@huntersonline.org

Please do not hesitate to write. I don’t mind offering some advice and guidance (at no cost) via email.

I am the Data Chief at the Virginia G. Piper Center for Personalized Diagnostics within the Biodesign Institute at Arizona State University.

As a professional computer programmer and database designer with degrees in biology and computer programming, I have been the co-author many scientific papers, including the following:

  • “The ORFeome Collaboration: a genome-scale human ORF-clone resource” (Nature Methods, 25 February 2016) [article]
  • “Exploration of panviral proteome: high-throughput cloning and functional implications in virus-host interactions.” (Theranostics, 6 June 2014) [article]
  • “DNASU plasmid and PSI:Biology-Materials repositories: resources to accelerate biological research.” (Nucleic Acids Research, Jan. 2014) [article]
  • “PSI:Biology-materials repository: a biologist’s resource for protein expression plasmids” (Journal of Structural and Functional Genomics, 1 March 2011) [article]
  • “High-quality gene assembly directly from unpurified mixtures of microarray-synthesized oligonucleotides” (Nucleic Acids Research, Sep. 2010) [article]
  • “Genome-directed primers for selective labeling of bacterial transcripts for DNA microarray analysis” (Nature Biotechnology, Vol. 18, No. 6, 2000) [article]
